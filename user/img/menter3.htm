Stunning Marigold Flower Wedding Decoration IDEAS FOR [2020]

  • February 6, 2022

Marigold Flower Wedding Decoration is something that brings colors and brightness to any Indian wedding ceremony, whether it’s Jhoola decoration on Mehndi, flower Rangoli decor on Haldi.

There’s something about the diverse colors of this bright flower, whether golden, yellow or orange, that automatically creates a sensation of celebration! Every wedding venue’s air is filled with a genuine sense of joyful pleasure thanks to marigolds.

Flowers, without a doubt, never fail to give a touch of elegance to any setting. Now, a variety of gorgeous Flowers For Weddings such as roses, orchids, and lilies dazzle a variety of decors with their allure, but genda has something unique to offer. Isn’t that so? It’s also very Indian. We witness it bringing joy to our celebrations, from simple poojas to huge events that occur in our lives.

So, if you’re looking for some unique ways to include Marigold Flowers in your wedding ceremony, keep reading! Take a look at the Ideas soyou could just gain some inspiration for your own wedding dcor!

top marigold flower wedding decoration ideas

1) Marigold Jhoola Decor

2) Wedding Mandap

Now that’s a spot where you and your partner will start your adventure together. It will be carved in your mind for the rest of your life. When it comes to counting its importance in the portion, the genda, which is a constant in our holy pujaas, stands tall. However, you may use it in the most inventive ways to give your wedding mandap an unique touch.

3)Marigold Flower Wall Decoration.

Simple Marigold Flower Wedding Decoration ideas may sometimes go a long way! Consider creating a floral curtain on your venue wall with several streamers of brilliant yellow Marigolds. In fact, this may be a terrific way to decorate your walls. Surround the Marigold drapes with green foliage and ferns to provide an extra eco-friendly touch to your stage decor!

4) Genda Phool Rangoli or “Marigold Flower Decoration on Floor”

What would we do if we didn’t have our usual Marigold rangoli? We prefer to keep things simple. Marigold flowers are so brilliant that a large spread of Marigold on the floor may instantly brighten up our event.

5) Simple Marigold Flower Decoration for Home

Marigold flowers are a symbol of prosperity and joy. They are also popular for their bright colors, and they can be used as a wedding decoration. Marigolds can be used in both indoor and outdoor dcor, and they can be used to create beautiful arrangements. When you want to use them as a wedding decoration, it is best to use them sparingly because they will not last long if you put them everywhere.

6) Marigold Flower Entry

Surprise your guests with some out-of-the-box ideas for adorning your wedding venue entry point with some amazing genda phool dcor. Let’s get rid of the traditional marigold flower arrangement and replace it with a marigold tree, a flower fountain, and a marigold tree.

7) Marigold Dome With Drapes

Try this Marigold Flower Wedding Decoration at the entry gate. The marigold flowers gathered together to form a dome-shaped structure look just suitable for your wedding. The white flower streamers dangling from the ceiling and the pale yellow dupatta drape adornment complete the whole appearance.

8) Marigold Photobooth

It’s always exciting to offer you wedding dcor trends that are setting the bar. This time, we tried to compile a list of Marigold flower decoration ideas for you.
